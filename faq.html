Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Maile Fresh Laundry services.

Got Questions?

We Have Answers

What are your hours? +

Our self-service floor is open Monday through Saturday 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sunday 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. The drop-off desk for wash-and-fold, dry cleaning, and alterations is staffed Monday–Friday 8 AM–6 PM, Friday–Saturday 8 AM–7 PM, and Sunday 9 AM–5 PM.

How does Wash & Fold work? +

Bring your laundry in a bag or hamper. We weigh it, tag it with your name, and wash, dry, and fold everything. Standard turnaround is 24 hours. Same-day rush (ready by 6 PM) is available for drop-offs before 10 AM. Pickup is during drop-off desk hours.

Do I need quarters for the machines? +

No quarters needed! All our machines are operated with a reloadable Maile Fresh laundry card. You can purchase or reload your card at our front kiosk with cash or credit card. Minimum load is $5.

What sizes are your washers? +

We have three washer sizes: small (up to 20 lbs), large (up to 50 lbs), and extra-large (up to 80 lbs). Perfect for everything from a single person's weekly laundry to bulky comforters and beach towels.

Can you handle my aloha shirts? +

Absolutely — it's our specialty! We know how to care for Hawaiian prints, rayon blends, and vintage aloha shirts. Our dry cleaning service is ideal for delicate or older aloha shirts. We'll pre-treat stains and use gentle cycles to preserve colors and fabric.

How long does dry cleaning take? +

Standard dry cleaning is a 48-hour turnaround. We send it to our partnered dry cleaning facility and have it back for you beautifully pressed in a garment bag. Rush dry cleaning (24 hours) is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer alterations? +

Yes! Our on-site seamstress Rosie has over 20 years of experience. Services include hemming, zipper replacement, button repairs, taking in or letting out seams, and custom fit adjustments. Free estimates are available — just bring your garment in.

Do you offer eco-friendly options? +

Yes! We offer cold-water wash options on all machines, which saves energy and is gentler on fabrics. Our Wash & Fold service uses phosphate-free, biodegradable detergents by default. You can also bring your own preferred detergent at no extra charge.

Is parking available? +

Yes — we have a free private parking lot right in front of our Farrington Hwy location. There's space for about 20 cars, and we also have a drop-off lane for quick wash-and-fold drop-offs so you don't even need to park.

Do you have Wi-Fi? +

Yes! Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all customers. We also have comfortable seating, a charging station, a small coffee station (weekday mornings), and a kids' corner to keep little ones entertained while you wait.

Still Have Questions?

Call us or stop by — we're always happy to help with a smile and a little aloha.
