Our Story

Born from Aloha, Built for Community

When Leilani and Koa Maka opened Maile Fresh in 2018, they wanted more than just a laundromat — they wanted a neighborhood gathering place where people could relax, chat, and leave feeling refreshed.

Named after the beloved maile vine, a symbol of love and respect in Hawaiian culture, Maile Fresh is deeply rooted in the Waipahu community.
